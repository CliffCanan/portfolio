About Me

I built Nooch - a P2P moible payments app - after graduating from Duke with too many friends who never paid me back. I taught myself to code and love to build clean, usable web and iOS apps. Also co-founded Rent Scene, a "digital brokerage" using technology to automate the apartment hunting experience.

When not reading up on state Money Transmitter laws, I'm an ambidextrous ping-pong player, political junkie, colorful rhetorician, Wentz-Wagon rider, and an Eagle Scout.
